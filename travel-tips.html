Travel Tips

What to Pack

  • Pack light, but include essentials
  • Dress for the weather
  • Don't forget sunscreen!

Local Customs

  • Respect local traditions
  • Learn a few basic phrases in Malay
  • Take off your shoes before entering someone's home

Safety Tips

  • Stay hydrated
  • Keep your belongings secure
  • Use reliable transportation

Currency Exchange Tips

  • Compare exchange rates from different providers
  • Avoid exchanging money at airports for better rates
  • Be cautious of hidden fees or commissions
  • Consider using a credit card with no foreign transaction fees
  • Exchange a small amount initially to test the service and rates
